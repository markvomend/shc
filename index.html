Our Services

We provide a full range of home care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual and family we serve.

Personal Care

Compassionate assistance with daily activities including bathing, grooming, dressing, mobility support, and medication reminders — preserving dignity and independence.

Companion Care

Meaningful companionship, engaging conversation, meal preparation, light housekeeping, and errand assistance to brighten every day.

Dementia Care

Specialized, patient support for individuals living with Alzheimer's and dementia. Our trained caregivers create safe, structured, and comforting environments.

Respite Care

Temporary relief for family caregivers so you can rest and recharge, knowing your loved one is in capable and caring hands.

24-Hour Care

Around-the-clock care and supervision for those who need continuous support, providing families peace of mind day and night.

About The Shepherd Home Care

At The Shepherd Home Care, our mission is simple: to serve seniors and their families with compassion, dignity, and trust.

Our founder, Mina Alexander, has spent more than 14 years serving the senior community across Northern Virginia. The journey into healthcare began with studying to become an EMT and volunteering to help save lives — an experience that built a deep respect for caring for others during some of their most vulnerable moments.

Over the years, that calling grew into a career working across many areas of senior care — including Durable Medical Equipment, Assisted Living communities, Home Care, and Home Health. But this work is not only professional; it is deeply personal.

At the age of 16, Mina became the primary caregiver for his father after a major accident left him disabled. That experience shaped a lifelong understanding of what families go through when caring for a loved one at home. Today, he continues to proudly serve and care for both of his parents, who live with him.

The Shepherd Home Care is a family-owned agency, built on the belief that care should never feel transactional. We take the time to truly know the people we serve — every client by name, every story, every caregiver valued.
